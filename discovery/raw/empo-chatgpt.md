# Empo Tournament Game Inventory

## Scope and method

This pass was limited to Smogon’s Tier-1 singles officials and team tours named in the brief: Smogon Tour, Official Smogon Tournament, Official Ladder Tournament, Smogon Classic, Smogon Grand Slam, Smogon Masters, World Cup of Pokémon, Smogon Premier League, Smogon Champions League, and Smogon Snake Draft. I used Smogon’s Tournament Statistics Archive plus tournament-specific replay/resource threads and replay archives, then expanded only links that were literally present on pages I opened. Where a game was visibly listed but I did not or could not resolve the replay URL, I logged it with `"url": null` rather than guessing. citeturn15view0turn17view0turn21view1turn21view2turn21view3turn21view4turn21view5turn34view4turn38view0turn30view0turn27view0

## Career map

Empo’s first recoverable Tier-1 footprint in the material I opened appears in 2017, when he is present in Smogon Tour playoff-archive material and in OLT IV playoff/replay material. By 2018, his archival trail expands across OLT V, WCoP 2018, Snake II, and ORAS Championship 2018. By 2019, that expands again through WCoP 2019, OLT VI–VII, Snake III, and SPL-era material. citeturn18view0turn23search0turn23search1turn35view7turn37view7turn33view0turn35view6turn22view5turn37view6turn31search9

## Tournament tallies and availability

From the opened archives, Empo is directly recoverable in Smogon Tour playoff/archive material across multiple seasons, including the trophy seasons XXX, XXXIII, and XXXVI; in OLT replay threads from at least VII through XI; in WCoP replay threads in 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2024, and 2025; in SPL XI and SPL XII replay/resource threads; in Snake II and Snake III replay threads; in ORAS Championship 2018; and in the SS/SM official resource threads that carry later OST and WCoP material. citeturn18view0turn20search5turn24search25turn22view5turn22view4turn22view3turn22view2turn22view1turn35view7turn35view6turn35view5turn35view4turn35view3turn35view1turn35view0turn41view0turn30view0turn37view7turn37view6turn33view0turn27view0turn28view0

That is a coverage fact about the opened archive surfaces, not proof of zero participation elsewhere in forum round threads. citeturn18view1turn18view2turn37view0turn37view1turn37view2turn37view3turn37view4

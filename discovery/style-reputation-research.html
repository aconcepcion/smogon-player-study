Smogon player study · reputation research

Who are the most aggressive among 16 Smogon greats?

Ranked by documented reputation for aggressive, proactive, offensive play — the “Mikhail Tal of Pokémon” archetype. Style = teams + initiative + risk appetite + game length, not forum persona.

Method Top 3 Runners-up Style map Study pick Sources

Method

Definition of aggressive: offense / HO / momentum over balance–fat–stall; forces the action (doubles, hard reads, high tempo); calculated high-variance lines; short-to-mid wins via pressure — not 80-turn grinds.

Evidence classes: SELF · PEER · COMMENTATOR · TEAM. Primary spine: Smogon’s 20th-anniversary Top 10 (2024); interviews and prediction threads fill gaps. Research date: 12 Jul 2026.

Ranked top 3

#1 · Empo

Empo

Confidence: high Era: ORAS ladder → SM peak (2018–22) → still elite through SV
Aggression case
  • COMMENTATOR (Top 10, 2024): “major focus on risk and reward, Empo plays aggressively, rarely giving his opponents an opening to breathe.” Same piece: wins by “aggressive and clever plays.” Cleanest Tal framing on the list — tempo and variance, not just “uses HO.”
  • COMMENTATOR (OLT XI discussion, 2024): “Empo played this matchup with a good combination of aggressive pressure and defensive reads, never letting [opponent] get the momentum.” Live tour coverage, not vibes.
  • TEAM (SM sample archive): Empo-linked structures skew offense — e.g. Medicham–Volcarona volt-turn, sand DD Tar + Ash-Gren, Z-Drill sand — not fat/stall samples.
  • PEER-adjacent SPL culture: Empo’s SM games (e.g. SPL X finals vs blunder) regularly framed as high-tempo slugfests; treated as the SM standard others prep around.
Counter-evidence: Not a pure HO monomaniac — 2024 tournament-feature Kyurem bulky offense and various BO pastes exist. Aggression is line selection and pace more than “always six glass cannons.” Early career quieter; reputation crystallizes post–SPL X (2019).
Harvestability: Excellent. Heavy official volume post-2013: multi–Smogon Tour trophies (incl. ST 36, 2024), OST, SPL/WCoP SM/SV, OLT. SmogTours replays from ~2018 onward dense; peak SM alone is a full corpus.
#2 · Tesung

Tesung

Confidence: high Era: XY / early ORAS (~2014–16) only — short peak
Aggression case
  • COMMENTATOR (Top 10, 2024): “innate genius, aggressive play”; “most potent when wielding offensive teams”; fanbase via “aggressive playstyle and in-battle instinct”; “bombastic plays.” Official profile sells him as the aggressive prodigy.
  • SELF (OU PotW #9, Dec 2014): “I suppose hyper offense is my favorite… the one I first gravitated to.” Also: tour experience taught him to “play aggressively.”
  • SELF (same interview): On bad matchups — “I tend to play much more aggressively, because I have to to win.”
  • TEAM / era lore: Rain offense innovator in early XY SPL; HO and creative offense builds.
Counter-evidence: SELF — in tournaments often preferred balance; favorite personal tour carry was a semistall paste. Peak is brief; almost no post-2016 signal.
Harvest note: Thin surviving official PS volume vs Empo — short window, older smogtours IDs, many links dead. Purest HO self+official combo; harvest weakest of top 3.
#3 · Vert

Vert

Confidence: high Era: SV (2022–) primarily
Aggression case
  • SELF (OU Metagame Interview #2, Apr 2023): OST win powered by “heavily offensive selection.” Explicit: “Offense just gets the job in SV.”
  • SELF (same): “Offense is still good” post–Shed Tail; lives in the offense builder vs fat unaware cores.
  • TEAM: Innovator of Glimmora + Ting-Lu offense, sun variants, shed-tail spam — momentum structures, not stall.
  • PEER (SPL XVII SM, 2025/26): Opponents prep for “vert’s offense brings” as the known load pattern.
Counter-evidence: Self admits variety to avoid counterteams (Scream Tail + Dondozo, Electric Terrain, sun) — offense-biased, not mono-HO autopilot. Reputation is generation-local; thin public style record pre-SV.

Runners-up

McMeghan — mid aggression / creative BO (not Tal)

SELF (OU PotW #2, Oct 2014): favorites are “Balance and Bulky Offense”; stall too matchup-reliant, HO too “guess-reliant.” Sample team he posts is standard BO. Top 10 (2024) stresses longevity and unconventional choices — not pure offense. High creativity and can pilot offense, but self-map is mid-tempo BO. Confidence: medium-high on not top-3 pure aggression. Era: BW→SV longevity.

CTC — builder range; self-stall undercuts Tal ranking

Famous for eclectic archives and later HO ladder work (e.g. Ape No Kill Ape HO with CBB, 2022). SELF (OU PotW #10, Dec 2014): if forced to pick → “full fuckin stall w Chansey Skarm.” Loud forum persona ≠ offensive gameplan. Treat as flexible builder, not attack-minded pilot. Confidence: medium. Era: BW2–XY peak builder; longer residual presence.

Study recommendation

For attack-minded study with a real corpus: Empo.
Documented risk/tempo aggression at the highest official level, still producing games in the Showdown era, offense-leaning sample history without being a one-dimensional HO bot.

Tesung is the purer “favorite = HO + aggressive instincts” package if you accept a short, partially dead-link archive.
Vert is the best pure modern offense monographer if you only care about SV.
